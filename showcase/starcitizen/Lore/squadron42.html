Squadron 42

Squadron 42 is the legendary volunteer unit assigned to the UEE's Paul Steed (CV 023) of the 2nd Fleet of the United Empire of Earth Navy. It can be found in many warzones and even took part in the Second Tevarin War's infamous Battle of Centauri in 2610. Those who enlist with the 42nd Squadron may earn UEE Citizenship.
