Administration Facility

The Administration Facility is a new building at the Kerbal Space Center and became available with the release of update 0.25.

Purpose

The Administration Facility is a new facility at the KSC which will let the player to choose from different strategies to boost certain aspects of the program. For example, science gains can be boosted at the expense of the reputation gains or vice versa. There are currently eight strategies available to players.
