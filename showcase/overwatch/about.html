About

Overwatch, a game developed by Blizzard Entertainment, has managed to gain quite some populariy and interest even before it was officialy released. Some of its acclaimed features are the colorful and distinct artstlye and a wide selection of heroes to choose from which are all suited for different playstlyes.

This website is created by Peter Cserei and Gabriël Wessels Beljaars. We are a duo of 2 students from the Avans in Den Bosch, the Netherlands. We were asked to develop a website using the knowledge end experience we have learned in the Webdesign courses.

We hope that this website is able to aid you during your first days in the exciting world of Overwatch
