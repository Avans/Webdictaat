About us

Starting Team Fortress 2 can be a daunting task for new players and we hope that this website clears up and explains some of the basics in simple terms. Team Fortress is an amazingly fun and challenging game, especially if you find some friends to play with

This website is created by Peter Askey, Milan Damen and Gabriël Wessels Beljaars. We are a group of 3 students from the Avans Highschool in Den Bosch ,the Netherlands. We were asked to develop a website using the knowledge we have learned in the Webdesign courses.

We decided to create a website based on the game Team Fortress 2. The main reasons for this choice is because we three all have some knowledge about this game and because it has a fairly substantial player base. This website is the fruit of our labours, and we hope you enjoy your stay and learn a lot.
